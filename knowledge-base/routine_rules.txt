# SkinNavi - Skincare Routine Rules & Guidelines

1. BASIC ROUTINE STRUCTURE
Morning (AM) - Focus on Protection:
- Step 1: Cleanser (Gentle)
- Step 2: Toner / Essence
- Step 3: Serum (Antioxidant, e.g., Vitamin C)
- Step 4: Moisturizer
- Step 5: Sunscreen (Crucial step)

Evening (PM) - Focus on Repair & Treatment:
- Step 1: Makeup Remover / Double Cleanse
- Step 2: Cleanser
- Step 3: Exfoliant (AHA/BHA) / Clay Mask (1-3 times a week, not daily)
- Step 4: Toner / Essence
- Step 5: Serum / Treatment (e.g., Retinol, Niacinamide)
- Step 6: Moisturizer (Can be thicker than AM)
- Step 7: Spot Treatment (If needed)

2. LAYERING RULES
- Rule of Consistency: Apply from Thinnest to Thickest (Water-based -> Gel -> Lotion -> Cream -> Oil).
- Rule of Solubility: Water before Oil.
- pH Dependency: Apply low pH products (Vitamin C, AHA, BHA) immediately after cleansing, before higher pH products.
- Wait Times: Wait 15-20 minutes after applying strong actives (AHA, BHA, Retinol) before applying the next step to allow them to work at their optimal pH.

3. INGREDIENT CONFLICTS (DO NOT MIX AT THE SAME TIME)
- ❌ Retinol + AHA/BHA: Causes severe irritation, dryness, and compromises the skin barrier. (Alternative: Use AHA/BHA in AM and Retinol in PM, or alternate nights).
- ❌ Vitamin C (L-AA) + Niacinamide (High Concentration): Can cause flushing and potentially diminish the efficacy of Vitamin C. (Alternative: Space out 15-20 mins, or use Vitamin C in AM and Niacinamide in PM).
- ❌ Retinol + Benzoyl Peroxide: Benzoyl Peroxide can oxidize Retinol, making it less effective, and combining them causes extreme dryness.
- ❌ Multiple Exfoliants: Avoid using physical scrubs and chemical exfoliants (AHA/BHA) in the same routine.

4. BEST PAIRINGS (SYNERGISTIC EFFECTS)
- ✅ Vitamin C + Sunscreen: Enhances protection against UV rays and free radicals.
- ✅ Retinol + Niacinamide: Niacinamide helps soothe the skin and reduce the irritation often caused by Retinol.
- ✅ Hyaluronic Acid + Moisturizer (Occlusive): Hyaluronic Acid draws moisture in, while the moisturizer locks it into the skin.
- ✅ AHA/BHA + Hyaluronic Acid: Exfoliating first allows hydrating ingredients to penetrate better.

5. GENERAL GOLDEN RULES
- Patch Test: Always patch test new products on a small area of skin (jawline or inner arm) for 24-48 hours before full-face application.
- One at a Time: Introduce only one new product at a time to identify any potential irritants.
- Sunscreen is Non-Negotiable: Must be applied every day, regardless of weather or indoor/outdoor status.
- Listen to Your Skin: If your skin is red, peeling, or stinging, stop all actives and focus on hydration and barrier repair.
- Consistency > Complexity: A simple 3-step routine done daily is more effective than a 10-step routine done inconsistently.
